# Product

## Register

product

## Users

QuestLife administrators and super admins who manage quests, review publication requests, curate featured content, maintain adventure packs, and administer internal access from a private web dashboard.

## Product Purpose

QuestLife Admin is the operational control surface for keeping app content accurate, publishable, and well-governed. Success means admins can move through repeated workflows quickly, understand their permissions, and act without visual clutter or broken navigation.

## Brand Personality

Focused, confident, polished. The dashboard should feel like a dependable operations tool while preserving the QuestLife identity through restrained color, clear icons, and energetic but practical hierarchy.

## Anti-references

Do not make the admin app feel like a marketing landing page, decorative card showcase, or experimental navigation concept. Avoid clipped controls, oversized collapsed navigation, awkward side-stripe selection states, and any layout that hides primary actions at normal browser zoom.

## Design Principles

- Keep navigation predictable, compact, and always usable at normal desktop zoom.
- Let the active route read as a clean selected control, not a cut-off decoration.
- Preserve task density for admin workflows while leaving enough space for scanning.
- Keep bottom utility actions visible and separate from the scrollable navigation list.
- Prefer familiar dashboard patterns over novelty when managing operational content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, 44px minimum interactive targets, clear selected states that do not rely on color alone, and predictable keyboard/mouse behavior. Avoid hiding core controls behind viewport height assumptions.
